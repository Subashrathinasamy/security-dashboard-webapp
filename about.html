About Us

Welcome to KOSO India Pvt. Ltd., where innovation meets excellence in delivering trusted security and industrial solutions

Company Overview

Founded in 1990, KOSO India Pvt. Ltd. is headquartered in Mumbai, serving clients across the globe. Our mission is to provide robust and reliable security solutions that protect businesses and their assets with cutting-edge technology.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the leading provider of innovative security systems worldwide.

Mission: Deliver exceptional security monitoring services with integrity, efficiency, and customer-centric solutions.

Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Customer First, Excellence, and Collaboration guide every decision we make, ensuring trust and quality in all our services.

What We Do / Services

We specialize in cloud-based security dashboards, multi-factor authentication systems, and real-time data monitoring. Our solutions cater to industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and corporate enterprises.

Infrastructure & Capabilities

Equipped with state-of-the-art data centers and cutting-edge analytics tools, we leverage proprietary algorithms and secure cloud infrastructure to deliver uninterrupted services.

Certifications & Quality Assurance

KOSO India Pvt. Ltd. holds ISO 27001 certification and follows stringent quality control processes to guarantee the highest security standards.

Innovation & R&D

We continuously invest in R&D, developing patented security algorithms and collaborating with top technology partners to stay ahead in the industry.

Sustainability & CSR

Committed to environmental sustainability, we implement energy-efficient operations and support community programs focused on education and digital literacy.

Global Footprint

With offices and service centers in Asia, Europe, and North America, KOSO proudly serves clients in over 70 countries worldwide.
