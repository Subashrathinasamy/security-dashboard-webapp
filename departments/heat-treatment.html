Heat Treatment Department

The Heat Treatment Department applies controlled heating and cooling to improve metal properties like hardness, strength, and ductility.

Heat Treatment Processes

Common processes include annealing, quenching, tempering, and normalizing. Each method alters the metal's microstructure to achieve desired mechanical properties.

Equipment Used

The department uses furnaces, quenching tanks, and temperature control systems to perform precise heat cycles. Automation ensures consistency and quality control.

Quality Control and Safety

Strict monitoring of temperatures and timings is critical. Safety protocols are followed to protect workers from heat hazards and ensure safe handling of hot components.

Impact on Casting Quality

Heat treatment enhances mechanical properties, reduces residual stresses, and improves wear resistance, directly affecting the durability and performance of cast products.
