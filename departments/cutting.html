Cutting Department

The Cutting Department is responsible for trimming and cutting castings to remove excess material and prepare them for finishing operations.

Cutting Processes

The department uses various cutting methods including sawing, grinding, and abrasive cutting to achieve precise shapes and dimensions.

Equipment and Tools

Common tools include band saws, abrasive wheels, and CNC cutting machines. These ensure accuracy and efficiency in removing excess material.

Quality and Safety

Operators follow strict safety protocols due to hazards such as flying debris and sharp edges. Quality checks ensure cut surfaces meet specifications.

Role in Production

The cutting department prepares castings for subsequent finishing processes such as grinding, polishing, and inspection, contributing to overall product quality.
