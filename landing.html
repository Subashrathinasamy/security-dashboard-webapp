About Us

KOSO India Pvt. Ltd. stands as a premier steel foundry and precision engineering company, committed to delivering high-quality components that power critical industrial applications worldwide. With decades of experience and a robust infrastructure, KOSO combines traditional steel casting expertise with cutting-edge manufacturing technologies, including CNC machining and automated quality control systems. Our foundry specializes in producing complex steel castings that meet stringent standards for strength, durability, and dimensional accuracy. We serve diverse sectors such as oil & gas, power generation, petrochemicals, and heavy machinery, where reliability under extreme conditions is paramount. At KOSO, our highly skilled team works closely with clients from design through production, ensuring tailored solutions that optimize performance and minimize downtime. Driven by innovation and sustainability, KOSO’s foundry operations prioritize eco-friendly practices and energy-efficient processes. Our state-of-the-art Nashik facility integrates advanced metallurgical testing and heat-treatment capabilities, enabling us to deliver components that not only meet but exceed global quality certifications. As part of the global Nihon Koso Group, KOSO India Pvt. Ltd. embodies a legacy of excellence and continuous improvement, striving to be the trusted partner in steel foundry solutions that empower industries to advance safely and efficiently.

Infrastructure & Technology

KOSO India Pvt. Ltd. operates a modern, fully-equipped steel foundry in Nashik, designed to meet global engineering standards. Our facility features automated casting lines, CNC machining centers, and advanced inspection systems that ensure precision and consistency in every product. With capabilities ranging from precision casting to high-accuracy machining, we produce critical components like control valves and actuators for demanding industrial applications. Our quality labs conduct rigorous testing to maintain international certifications such as ISO 9001:2015 and PED. We also embrace smart technologies and energy-efficient systems to boost productivity while minimizing environmental impact. Through innovation and robust infrastructure, KOSO delivers reliable, high-performance solutions worldwide.
